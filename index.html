Serving Informs Everything We Do

At Calvary, we believe service is integral to fully becoming who God created us to be. This is accomplished through gifts-based service. This means that we desire for everyone to identify their gifts and passions and to use those to build up the church for the glory of God. We have found that one of the best ways to build relationships and strengthen your connection with church life is to serve alongside one another.
